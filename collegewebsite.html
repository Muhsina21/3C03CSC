Nehru Arts and Science College Kanhangad

About Us

Early in 1964, the enlightened public of Kanhangad and Nileswar resolved to honour the memory of Jawaharlal Nehru, the architect of modern India by undertaking educational and cultural activities in this educationally backward area.In pursuit of this idea,a dedicated and learned team set up on 1966 ,an educational society known as "The Nehru Memmorial Education Society" under the distinguished presidentship of the late Shri.C.K.Nair following which the society was duly registered.The establishment of Nehru Arts and Science College,Kanhangad in thos backward coastal tract was the major task the society had set before themselves and had subsequently accomplished it.
